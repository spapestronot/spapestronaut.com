Who is Spapestronaut

2026-03-27

Just someone figuring things out, one step at a time.

I started this site because I wanted a space that was truly mine — no algorithms, no themes I didn't choose, no platform telling me how to present my thoughts. Just words on a page, as simple as it gets.

Why "The Shelf"?

A shelf holds things you care about. Books you've read. Objects that mean something. Ideas you're not done with yet. That's what this is — a place to put things down so I can come back to them.

What You'll Find Here

Honest thinking about whatever I'm exploring at the moment. Could be:

  • Building things from scratch
  • Financial freedom and what it actually means
  • Learning in public, even when it's messy
  • Whatever else earns a spot on the shelf

Everything you do matters. That's not a slogan — it's a reminder to myself. Every small choice compounds. This site is one of those choices.
